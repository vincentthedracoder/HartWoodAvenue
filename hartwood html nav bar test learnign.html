About me

I’m Shannon and I’m the maker behind Hartwood Avenue. I am a Southern girl with a New Englander’s heart as I have now lived below the mason Dixon line for just over half my life. I’m a lot of “hey ya’lls” and a bit of “where’d we pahk the cah?” Like myself, my creations are a mash up. A little bit farmhouse and a little bit Bohemian. I introduce to you “Farmhemian”! Everything you see here is made in my home studio. I even do my own wood working out in the garage! It’s an honor to create home decor from the heart for you. Thank ya’ll for stopping by!
